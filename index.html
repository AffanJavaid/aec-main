Frequently Asked Questions

Which country is best for my profile?

We evaluate your academics, budget, test scores, and goals, then recommend matches across Australia, UK, USA, Canada, and Europe.

Can you help with scholarships?

Yes. We shortlist university and government scholarships and refine your SOP to increase odds.

What is GTE (Australia)?

Genuine Temporary Entrant is a statement and evidence to show you intend to study in Australia temporarily. We help you compile it correctly.

How long does visa processing take?

Timelines vary by country and intake. We'll set expectations after your assessment and application submission.
